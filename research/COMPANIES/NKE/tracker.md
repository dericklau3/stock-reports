## Current research view

**Neutral / wait for confirmation**。

NKE 是全球运动品牌龙头，但当前处在经营低谷和战略修复期。43.21 美元附近已经进入可研究区，但不是高确定性买点。核心问题是 EPS 能否从 1.5-2.1 美元区间恢复到 2.5-3.25 美元。

## Current main thesis

- Nike、Jordan、Converse 仍拥有全球品牌资产、运动员资源和分销网络。
- FY2026 收入基本持平但汇率中性 -2%，Q4 revenue -1% / currency-neutral -4%，说明复苏尚未确认。
- Wholesale 正修复，Direct 仍弱：FY2026 Wholesale +6% / currency-neutral +4%；NIKE Direct -6% / currency-neutral -8%。
- Greater China 和 Converse 是主要拖累项。
- 当前估值已不高估，但仍依赖利润恢复；若 FY2027 EPS 只有约 1.72，forward PE 约 25x，并不便宜。

## Core thesis assumptions that must remain true

- Nike brand remains culturally relevant globally.
- Product innovation in performance categories can restore consumer demand.
- Wholesale repair does not become channel stuffing.
- Direct channel can stabilize without excessive promotions.
- Greater China decline is cyclical/competitive but not permanent brand impairment.
- Balance sheet remains strong enough to protect dividends while reinvesting.

## Red-line conditions

Review/downgrade if any of the following occur：

- FY2027 EPS expectation falls below 1.50 without credible recovery plan。
- Dividend payout exceeds 100% of earnings for more than a short transition period。
- Greater China continues double-digit currency-neutral decline beyond FY2027。
- NIKE Direct digital revenue continues double-digit decline。
- Inventory rises while revenue declines。
- Gross margin excluding one-time tariff recovery deteriorates further。
- Management relies on vague brand language without segment-level progress.

## Management promises / strategic milestones to track

- Sport Offense execution by sport category。
- Product portfolio rebalancing and reduction of excess supply in selected footwear products。
- Repositioning Nike Brand Digital as a full-price platform。
- Reinvestment in wholesale distribution and elevated physical retail presentation。
- Increased demand creation / brand marketing effectiveness。
- Cost realignment across supply chain and technology.

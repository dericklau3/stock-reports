## Current research stance

- **View**: Neutral / wait for confirmation（中性，等待 FY2026 Q4 与 FY2027 订单兑现）
- **Confidence**: B-
- **Core conclusion**: LITE is a real AI optical-interconnect beneficiary with exceptional FY2026 revenue growth and margin expansion, but the current price already discounts a strong FY2027/FY2028 OCS/CPO/cloud-optics bull case. It deserves close tracking but lacks traditional margin of safety at the current price.
- **Best use of this memo**: Track FY2026 Q4 earnings on 2026-08-11, FY2027 guidance, OCS backlog, CPO delivery, FCF conversion and dilution from convertibles/preferred stock.

## Key thesis

Lumentum sells photonic chips, laser components, modules and optical subsystems that enable high-bandwidth AI/cloud data-center connectivity. FY2026 results show a genuine inflection: Q1-Q3 revenue accelerated from $533.8M to $808.4M and Q4 guidance is $960M-$1.01B. The issue is not whether the business is improving; the issue is whether the current ~$55B equity value can be justified by future OCS/CPO/cloud-optics growth after dilution and FCF conversion.

## Risks to track

1. Valuation compression if growth or FY2027 guidance slows.
2. Customer concentration and limited purchase commitments.
3. Optical hardware price competition and ASP pressure.
4. OCS/CPO backlog/orders may not convert to durable high-margin revenue.
5. Convertible notes and Series A convertible preferred stock create dilution and capital-structure complexity.
6. FCF may lag non-GAAP EPS due to capex, inventory and receivables.
7. Tariffs, export controls, rare earth restrictions and global supply-chain risk.
8. Industrial laser and 3D sensing demand may remain cyclical or weak.

## Red-line conditions

- FY2026 Q4 revenue or margin misses guidance.
- FY2027 guide does not imply meaningful growth from Q4 annualized run-rate.
- OCS backlog stops expanding or conversion timing slips materially.
- CPO 1H calendar 2027 order is delayed, resized down or fails margin expectations.
- GAAP/non-GAAP gap widens while FCF remains weak.
- Fully diluted share count rises materially faster than revenue and FCF per share.
- Top customer orders slow, get canceled, or show inventory digestion.

## What would change the view

- **Upgrade to Constructive/Positive**: Price falls into a more reasonable zone while FY2027 revenue/order visibility improves, OCS/CPO turn into quantifiable high-margin revenue, and FCF margin rises meaningfully.
- **Downgrade to Negative**: Q4/FY2027 guide disappoints, customer concentration risk materializes, gross margin declines, OCS/CPO delays occur, or dilution/FCF weakness overwhelms revenue growth.

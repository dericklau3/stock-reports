## Current research view

**Constructive but watchful** — Cloudflare 是高质量、高成长、稀缺的平台型互联网基础设施公司，但 2026-07-07 收盘价 **268.83 美元**附近估值已经非常满，当前更适合长期跟踪，不适合按安全边际逻辑追高。

## Current main thesis

Cloudflare 正从 CDN / DDoS / Web security 公司升级为统一的 connectivity cloud：网络、安全、Zero Trust、开发者平台、边缘计算、AI Gateway / Workers AI 都跑在同一张全球网络上。若 AI-native Internet、agentic apps、API 安全和边缘计算持续发展，Cloudflare 有机会成为关键基础设施节点。

## Key risks

1. **Valuation compression**: biggest risk. Current valuation already prices in platform success and sustained 30%+ growth.
2. **AI-first restructuring execution**: 20% workforce reduction may improve efficiency or damage sales/product/customer execution.
3. **Gross margin pressure**: AI inference, edge compute, storage, and network costs may pressure 70%+ gross margin.
4. **SBC and dilution**: Q1 SBC and related payroll taxes were about $127.5M, roughly 20% of revenue.
5. **Competition**: AWS, Microsoft, Google, Zscaler, Palo Alto, Akamai, Fastly, Vercel and others compete across product lines.
6. **Narrative risk**: AI / Workers / R2 optionality may not translate into disclosed revenue fast enough to justify current multiples.

## Core thesis assumptions that must remain true

- Revenue growth remains around or above 30% in the near term.
- DBNRR stays above 115%, ideally improves from 118%.
- Large customer count continues strong growth.
- Gross margin remains above 70% despite AI and edge infrastructure workloads.
- FCF margin improves toward 15%-20% over time.
- SBC / revenue trends down materially.
- Workers, R2, AI Gateway and Zero Trust become measurable revenue contributors, not only narrative optionality.

## Red-line conditions forcing review or downgrade

- Revenue growth falls below 25% without a clear temporary explanation.
- DBNRR falls below 110%.
- Gross margin continues to decline materially.
- FCF margin stalls or reverses despite revenue scale.
- SBC / revenue remains near 20% for multiple quarters.
- AI-first restructuring causes sales productivity, customer retention, or product execution issues.
- Stock trades above $310 without corresponding fundamental acceleration.

## Current research view

**中性 / 等待确认，但当前价格已经进入可严肃跟踪区间。** 2026-07-01 重新使用新版 `us-equity-research` 框架复盘后，CRCL 的结论从“只可观察”细化为：当前约 **$62.63** 落在基准合理区间中部偏上，不是明显泡沫，但也不是强安全边际买点。

## Current main thesis

Circle 的长期价值取决于能否从“稳定币储备利息型发行商”升级为“全球数字美元支付、结算和链上金融基础设施平台”。USDC 是核心资产，reserve income 是当前利润来源；Arc、CPN、StableFX、Agent Stack、USYC 是未来平台化选项。投资关键是这些选项能否转化为 CRCL 股东可享有的高质量每股现金流。

## Current valuation framework

当前价格：约 **$62.63**。

- Bear 情景：**$14–26**，相对当前 **-77% 到 -58%**。假设利率下降、USDC 增长放缓、RLDC margin 承压、平台收入慢。
- Base 情景：**$45–77**，相对当前 **-28% 到 +23%**。假设 USDC 增长抵消部分降息压力，RLDC margin 38%–40%，other revenue 稳步增长。
- Bull 情景：**$112–189**，相对当前 **+79% 到 +202%**。假设 USDC 持续高增，Arc/CPN 商业化，平台收入被重新定价。

安全边际判断：

- 强安全边际：**$35–45**。
- 可分批研究/观察：**$45–55**。
- 基准合理但不便宜：**$55–75**。
- 高于 **$80–90** 需要很强 bull-case 证据支撑。

## Key risks

- 利率下降压缩 reserve income。
- Coinbase、Binance 和生态伙伴拿走大量经济利益，RLDC margin 难以提升。
- USDC 市占率被 USDT、银行稳定币、PYUSD、DeFi 原生稳定币等挤压。
- Arc token / Arc network 的经济价值不一定明确归属于 CRCL 股东。
- SBC 和摊薄股数影响每股价值。
- 监管可能既提高门槛，也可能提高成本和限制收益分享。
- 当前价格对熊市情景的保护不足。

## Core thesis assumptions that must remain true

1. USDC remains a trusted, liquid, regulated dollar stablecoin with broad exchange, wallet, developer, and institutional adoption.
2. USDC average circulation continues to grow at a high rate through cycles.
3. RLDC margin remains around management guidance or improves.
4. Other revenue grows beyond a small percentage of total revenue.
5. Arc / CPN / Agent Stack create shareholder-relevant economics, not only ecosystem narrative.
6. SBC and dilution normalize after the IPO period.

## Red-line conditions

- RLDC margin falls materially below 38% without a credible strategic reason.
- USDC market share or average circulation declines for multiple quarters.
- Reserve return rate declines sharply while distribution costs remain high.
- Other revenue misses FY2026 guidance or fails to show durable growth.
- Arc token economics dilute or bypass CRCL shareholder value.
- Major USDC depeg, reserve, custody, compliance, AML, sanction, or regulatory failure.
- SBC/diluted shares rise enough that company-level growth does not translate into per-share value.

## Management promises / milestones to track

- Multi-year USDC in circulation 40% CAGR through cycle.
- FY2026 other revenue $150–170M.
- FY2026 RLDC margin 38–40%.
- FY2026 adjusted operating expenses $570–585M.
- Arc network progress after $222M ARC Token presale.
- CPN transaction volume growth beyond $8.3B annualized run-rate from Q1 2026 disclosure.

## What would change the research view

**Upgrade** if Circle proves non-interest platform revenue is scaling, RLDC margin stays stable, USDC grows strongly, Arc / CPN create clear shareholder economics, and valuation remains in or below the base-case range.

**Downgrade** if growth depends mainly on rate-sensitive reserve income, distribution costs worsen, USDC market share weakens, dilution/SBC offsets operating progress, or price rises above $80–90 without fundamental proof.

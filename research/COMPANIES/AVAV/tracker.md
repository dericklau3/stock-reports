# AeroVironment（AVAV）Tracker

Last updated: 2026-07-08

## Current Research View

**Constructive but watchful / 结构性看好，但需要估值纪律和财报确认。**

AVAV 是无人机、巡飞弹、反无人机和国防科技平台的高弹性标的。公司业务方向非常契合现代战争变化，但 FY2026 的收入增长被 BlueHalo 并购显著放大，GAAP 利润和自由现金流质量仍弱，FY2027 需要验证整合、产能扩张、订单转收入和 FCF 修复。

## Current Main Thesis

AVAV 的长期逻辑是：

`地缘冲突和现代战争形态变化 → 小型无人机/巡飞弹/反无人机需求增长 → 美国与盟友补库存和采购新型系统 → AVAV 的 Switchblade、Red Dragon、Titan、Puma、JUMP 20、P550、VAPOR、BlueHalo 空天/网络/定向能能力受益。`

公司已经从“小型无人机公司”升级为“无人系统 + 巡飞弹 + 反无人机 + 空天/网络/定向能”的国防科技平台。

## Core Assumptions That Must Remain True

1. Switchblade / Red Dragon / precision strike demand remains durable.
2. Counter-UAS demand grows as drone warfare expands.
3. Funded backlog converts into FY2027 revenue on schedule.
4. BlueHalo SCDE stops dragging margins and begins contributing profit.
5. Capex expansion improves capacity and revenue, not just cash burn.
6. U.S. and allied defense budgets continue funding unmanned systems and counter-UAS.

## Red-Line Conditions

Review or downgrade if:

- FY2027 revenue or adjusted EBITDA guidance is cut.
- Book-to-bill falls below 1 for multiple quarters.
- Funded backlog declines materially.
- SCDE remains negative adjusted EBITDA through FY2027.
- Free cash flow remains sharply negative after capex peak without backlog growth.
- New goodwill/intangible impairment emerges.
- Dilution or convertible-note impact becomes larger than expected.

## Current research view

**Constructive but valuation-stretched**。

PLTR 是业务质量极高、AI 落地数据极强的公司，但当前约 3221 亿美元市值、约 60x EV/TTM Sales、约 120x P/TTM FCF 已经定价大量未来成功。适合长期深度跟踪，但当前安全边际不足。

## Current main thesis

- Palantir 已从政府数据软件公司升级为企业级 AI 操作系统公司。
- AIP 正在把商业业务从叙事变成真实收入和合同增长。
- 政府业务继续高增长，尤其美国政府。
- 公司同时具备高速增长、GAAP 盈利、高 FCF margin、净现金资产负债表。
- 最大问题不是业务，而是估值已经要求 2026-2030 持续高增长和高利润率。

Reverse-engineering current EV：

- If 2030 FCF margin = 40% and terminal FCF multiple = 45x, PLTR needs 2030 revenue around 175 亿美元, implying about 22.7% CAGR from 2026E revenue of roughly 77 亿美元。
- This is possible but demanding; current valuation leaves limited room for execution errors。

## Key risks

1. Valuation compression even if fundamentals remain strong。
2. AIP demand growth slows after early adoption wave。
3. Cloud/data/AI platform competition from Microsoft, Databricks, Snowflake, ServiceNow, Salesforce, OpenAI, Anthropic and hyperscalers。
4. Government budget, political, procurement or contract cancellation risk。
5. SBC and share dilution reduce per-share compounding。
6. TCV/RDV are not guaranteed revenue because contracts may include options and termination rights。

## Core thesis assumptions that must remain true

- AIP continues turning pilots/workshops into production contracts。
- U.S. commercial remains the main growth engine。
- Government business remains sticky and expands with defense/intelligence AI demand。
- Gross margin and FCF margin stay structurally high。
- SBC as % of revenue keeps declining。
- Palantir remains differentiated versus cloud, data and AI platform competitors。

## Red-line conditions

Review/downgrade if：

- U.S. commercial growth falls below 50% and continues decelerating。
- Revenue guidance stops rising or is cut。
- RDV/TCV growth materially weakens。
- FCF margin falls below 35% without clear reinvestment rationale。
- SBC as % of revenue starts rising again。
- Diluted share count accelerates。
- Major government contract loss or political restriction emerges。
- AIP fails to move from pilots to broad production deployments。

## Current research view

**Constructive but watchful（建设性但必须观察）**。IBM 已从传统硬件/外包公司转向软件主导的混合云、企业 AI、自动化和关键基础设施公司；但 2026-07-14 披露的 Q2 初步结果显示，软件与主机相关交易仍有明显季度波动和执行风险。

## Current main thesis

1. **核心价值**：Software 是利润核心，Q1 2026 软件收入 $7.052B、毛利率 82.8%、分部利润率 29.8%；Red Hat、Automation、Data、Transaction Processing 是主要利润池。
2. **现金流底座**：FY2025 收入 $67.535B，简化 FCF 约 $12.1B；Q1 2026 公司仍指引全年 constant currency revenue growth >5% 和 FCF 同比增加约 $1B。
3. **Q2 风险重定价**：2026 Q2 preliminary 收入 $17.2B、仅 +1%；Software +5%、Infrastructure -7%；公司承认大型交易未按预期关闭，说明市场此前给的 AI/软件化溢价需要重新验证。
4. **估值框架**：2026-07-15 收盘 $211.20，市值约 $198.5B；若 2026 FCF $12-13B，股权 FCF yield 约 6.0%-6.5%。当前价格进入可研究区，但强安全边际更接近 $160-180。

## Key risks

- Q2 失误不是时点问题，而是软件/主机需求和销售执行趋势性恶化。
- Red Hat、Automation、Data 的增长被 Transaction Processing 和 Infrastructure 周期抵消。
- HashiCorp、Confluent、DataStax 等并购推高商誉、无形资产和 SBC，但未提升有机增长。
- Lightwell $5B commitment 和未来五年 $10B+ 量子投入回收期长，可能挤压 FCF。
- 净债务较高，Q1 2026 debt including IBM Financing debt $66.4B，cash/restricted cash/marketable securities $11.8B。

## Core thesis assumptions that must remain true

- Red Hat and broader Software can sustain at least mid-to-high single-digit growth after Q2 disruption.
- Transaction Processing and IBM Z weakness is largely timing/cycle rather than durable demand impairment.
- Consulting GenAI signings convert into software attach and high-quality recurring revenue, not just low-margin services.
- FCF remains around $12B+ and can grow despite dividends, acquisitions, SBC and long-dated quantum/AI/security investments.
- Management does not drift away from IBM's original mission of trusted enterprise technology infrastructure; Lightwell and quantum should reinforce that mission, not become unfocused capital sinks.

## Red-line conditions for thesis review / downgrade

- 2026 Q2 final call or 10-Q materially lowers FY2026 FCF guidance.
- Q3 2026 again shows large deal timing failures or Software growth below mid-single digits.
- Red Hat growth drops materially below recent double-digit/near-double-digit trend.
- Net debt rises while FCF or segment profit falls.
- Management cannot quantify Lightwell, AI or quantum milestones beyond customer logos and TAM language.

## Management promises / milestones to follow

- Q1 2026 promise: FY2026 constant currency revenue growth >5% and FCF increase about $1B YoY.
- Q2 2026 follow-up: full-year expectations to be discussed on 2026-07-22 call.
- z17: Q2 preliminary says z17 remains nearly 130% program-to-program vs z16 and customers representing 85% of installed MIPs maintained or grew capacity.
